say - Speak text aloud (text-to-speech)

Usage:
  say <text>                 Speak the given text
  say -v <voice> <text>      Speak with a specific voice

Arguments:
  text  - Text to speak aloud
  voice - Voice name (engine-specific)

Examples:
  say Hello, I am Veltro.
  say -v echo The task is complete.
  say I found 3 files matching your query.

Requires /n/speech (run speech9p first).
Configure via: echo 'voice <name>' > /n/speech/ctl
